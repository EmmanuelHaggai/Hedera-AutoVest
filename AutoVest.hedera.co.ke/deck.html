Vision

To make investing in African financial markets as easy as sending a message. AutoVest AI connects AI, blockchain, and mobile money into a unified on-chain ecosystem that democratizes wealth creation.

2. The Problem

Current Challenges

  • Over 90% of Kenyans are excluded from stock market participation due to complexity and minimum investment barriers.
  • Traditional brokers require manual onboarding, paperwork, and high fees.
  • Retail investors lack real-time analytics, AI tools, and trustless systems to guide decision-making.

Result: Financial inclusion remains low despite high smartphone and mobile money penetration.

3. The Solution (The Hook)

Hedera AutoVest AI — an AI-powered WhatsApp investment agent that lets anyone:

  • Trade tokenized NSE stocks instantly.
  • Receive AI-driven recommendations using live data and sentiment analysis.
  • Send, receive, and invest using HBAR and HKSH tokens.
  • Subscribe to automated trading strategies (daily, weekly, monthly).
  • Perform KYC and top-ups directly inside WhatsApp.

Simple. Smart. On-Chain.

6. Product / Architecture & TRL

Architecture Overview

  • WhatsApp Cloud API – Conversational AI interface.
  • AutoVest Backend (PHP + Python) – Transaction logic, AI orchestration.
  • TCN Neural Network (AI Engine) – Time-series stock prediction using calibrated sigmoid curve.
  • Hedera Token Service (HTS) – Tokenized NSE assets + HKSH stablecoin.
  • Hedera Consensus Service (HCS) – Trade receipts & audit trail.
  • Stripe + M-Pesa + Google Auth – Payments and identity verification.
